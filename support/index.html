Frequently Asked Questions

How does Tutorld work?

Tutorld uses AI to provide personalized English conversation practice. Simply speak with our AI tutor and receive instant feedback on your pronunciation, grammar, and fluency.

Is Tutorld free to use?

Tutorld offers both free and premium features. Download the app to explore what's available and choose the plan that works best for you.

What languages are supported?

Tutorld currently focuses on English learning. We support learners from various native language backgrounds.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly within the app settings. If you need assistance, contact us at support@tutorld.com.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. Please review our Privacy Policy for detailed information about how we handle your data.
