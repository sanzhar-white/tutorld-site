Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Tutorld?

Simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play, create your account, and start your learning journey with our AI tutor.

What features are available in the free version?

The free version includes basic AI tutoring, limited voice interactions, and access to core learning materials.

How do I upgrade to a premium subscription?

You can upgrade through the app's settings or contact our support team for assistance with billing questions.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take privacy and security seriously. All data is encrypted and we comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Having trouble with voice features?

Ensure you have granted microphone permissions in your device settings. If issues persist, try restarting the app or contact support.
